1. Introduction

Code Arena ("the Platform") is a competitive programming web application developed as part of the 42 school curriculum. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By creating an account and using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2. Data Controller

The Platform is maintained by the Code Arena development team (tjorge-l, tborges-, strodrig, hepereir) as part of the ft_transcendence project at 42 school.

For any questions or concerns regarding this policy or your data, please contact us through the 42 school internal network.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the Platform and provide you with a competitive programming experience. Below is a detailed breakdown of the categories of data we collect.

3.1 Account Data

Collected at registration to create and secure your account:

  • Login / username
  • Email address
  • Hashed password (encrypted with BCrypt — never stored in plain text)

3.2 Profile Data

Optional information you may provide to personalize your profile:

  • Avatar image (optional upload)
  • Biography text (optional)

3.3 Game Data

Generated through your participation in duels and competitive activities:

  • Duel history and match records
  • Code submissions made during duels
  • Scores and match results
  • Elo rating and ranking position
  • Win/loss statistics
  • League rank

3.4 Social Data

Generated through your use of social features on the Platform:

  • Friend lists and friend request status
  • Chat messages exchanged between users

3.5 Technical Data

Minimal technical data required for Platform operation:

  • Session tokens (JWT) for authentication
  • WebSocket connection metadata for real-time features

We do NOT collect IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or any analytics/tracking data. The Platform contains no third-party tracking scripts, pixels, or telemetry.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain your account on the Platform
  • To facilitate matchmaking, duels, and competitive ranking
  • To display your profile, statistics, and leaderboard position to other users
  • To enable social features including friends, chat, and notifications
  • To execute submitted code in a sandboxed environment (Judge0) for duel evaluation

We do not use your data for profiling, automated decision-making, or any purpose beyond the core functionality of the Platform.

5. Code Execution & Security

Code you submit during duels is executed in a fully isolated sandbox environment with strict resource limits to ensure safety and fairness:

  • 10 seconds maximum CPU execution time
  • 128 MB maximum memory allocation
  • No network access — sandboxed code cannot make external requests
  • Read-only filesystem — no persistent writes outside the sandbox

Submitted code is stored in the database as part of your match history for review and record-keeping purposes. It is not shared with third parties and is not used for any purpose beyond duel evaluation and match history display.

6. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database running in isolated Docker containers
  • Passwords are hashed using BCrypt and are never stored in plain text
  • All communication is encrypted using HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • JWT tokens are used for authentication with configurable expiration periods
  • Redis is used for session management and matchmaking queue data

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

The Platform is entirely self-hosted and does not integrate with external analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your data remains within our infrastructure at all times.

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active on the Platform.

As this is an educational project developed as part of the 42 school curriculum, all data may be permanently deleted when the project evaluation period concludes. Users will be notified in advance if a data deletion event is scheduled.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access — Request to view the personal data we hold about you
  • Right of Rectification — Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right of Erasure — Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right of Portability — Export your match history and profile data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact any member of the development team through the 42 school internal network.

10. Cookies

The Platform does not use cookies for tracking or analytics purposes.

JWT tokens are stored in browser local storage solely for authentication purposes. No third-party cookies or tracking mechanisms are employed.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the project evolves and new features are introduced. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date displayed at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
